What is VC-Certified?

VC-Certified (VCP Compliance Certification) is a certification program where VSO-accredited Conformity Assessment Bodies (CABs) verify and certify that your trading system, algorithm, or platform conforms to the rigorous technical specifications of the VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) v1.0. The certification scheme is developed and governed by the VeritasChain Standards Organization (VSO), an independent standards body.

This certification is fundamentally different from traditional "financial audits" or "operational audits." We technically verify through automated test suites whether the data generated by your system iscryptographically protected,tamper-proof, and maintainsaccurate time synchronization and numerical precision.

"Verify, Don't Trust."

We don't say "trust us." We say "verify us."

VC-Certified is the technical passport that enables that verification.
