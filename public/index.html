What is VeritasChain Protocol (VCP)?

VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) is an open standard specification for recording the "decision-making" and "execution results" of algorithmic and AI-driven trading in an immutable and verifiable format. VCP provides a cryptographically secured chain of evidence, ensuring compliance with international regulations including MiFID II, EU AI Act, and CAT (Consolidated Audit Trail), making it essential for RobTech, Audit, and Compliance.

VCP records:

  • Trading signals, orders, and executions
  • Active risk limits and controls
  • AI model versions and decision factors
  • Time-synchronized event timestamps

VeritasChain Certified (VC-Certified)

VCP Compliance Certification

VeritasChain Inc. hosts the VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) standard and runs the VeritasChain Certified (VC-Certified) program, a certification for algorithmic trading and AI-driven trading systems worldwide, promoting standardization in AI safety, RegTech, and FinTech industries.

VC-Certified validates that a trading system:

  • Implements the VCP event model correctly
  • Preserves hash-chain and Merkle-tree integrity
  • Maintains clock-sync requirements for its tier (Silver / Gold / Platinum)
