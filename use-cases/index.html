Preventing Disputes & Restoring Trust

After VCP Implementation

  • All trading events (SIG → ORD → ACK → EXE) cryptographically verifiable

Ensuring Best Execution & Compliant Audit Trails

Retail / CFD / FX Brokers

Challenges

  • Difficult to prove best execution compliance
  • Cannot maintain integrity of millions of order logs
  • High costs for RTS 6 / RTS 25 compliance

After VCP Implementation

  • All events recorded in tamper-evident hash chains
  • MiFID II compliant millisecond-precision timestamps
  • Evidence submission to regulators: days → minutes
"Reduce compliance costs while maximizing transparency"

Transparent Matching & Order Integrity

Centralized Exchanges & Dark Pools

Challenges

  • Matching engine internals are "black box"
  • Need to prove price priority compliance
  • Increasing international scrutiny on market manipulation

After VCP Implementation

  • Merkle proofs demonstrate state transitions before/after matching
  • External auditors can verify integrity with partial logs only
  • Enhanced international trust & competitive advantage in listing approvals
"Matching engine transparency — the next frontier"

Lightweight Proofs for On-Chain + Off-Chain Logic

DeFi Protocols / L2 Bridges

Challenges

  • Cannot prove off-chain operations
  • Bridge internal logic remains opaque
  • No way to trace "what happened" when funds are lost

After VCP Implementation

  • Merkle-root anchoring connects off-chain events to on-chain audits
  • Easy integration with smart contracts, L2s, and oracles
  • Enhanced security audit quality
"Bridging off-chain transparency to on-chain verification"
