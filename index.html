What is VeritasChain Protocol (VCP)?

VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) is an open standard specification for recording the "decision-making" and "execution results" of algorithmic and AI-driven trading in an immutable and verifiable format. VCP provides a cryptographically secured chain of evidence, ensuring compliance with international regulations including MiFID II, EU AI Act, and CAT (Consolidated Audit Trail), making it essential for RegTech, Audit, and Compliance.

VCP also records:

Unlike FIX, which records only orders and executions:

  • AI model versions and decision factors
  • Active risk limits and triggered controls
  • Time-synchronized (PTP / NTP) timestamps
  • Cryptographically sealed audit trails

This expands beyond FIX, which cannot record internal decision factors or risk states.
This makes VCP essential for MiFID II RTS 25, EU AI Act Article 12, and next-generation RegTech compliance.

Why Now?

Global regulators are shifting from "trust-based" to "verification-based" oversight of algorithmic and AI-driven trading.

VeritasChain Certified (VC-Certified)

VCP Compliance Certification

VeritasChain Inc. hosts the VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) standard and runs the VeritasChain Certified (VC-Certified) program, a certification for algorithmic trading and AI-driven trading systems worldwide, promoting standardization in AI safety, RegTech, and FinTech industries.

VC-Certified validates that a trading system:

  • Implements the full VCP event model (SIG ‚Üí ORD ‚Üí ACK ‚Üí EXE ‚Üí CLS)
  • Preserves hash-chain and Merkle-tree integrity across all events
  • Meets clock-synchronization requirements for its tier (Silver / Gold / Platinum)
  • Records AI model versions, decision factors, and risk parameters

VC-Certified ensures that trading systems cannot falsify, alter, or omit audit data.

VC-Certified includes automated conformance tests (CTS) covering all VCP modules: VCP-CORE, VCP-TRADE, VCP-GOV, VCP-RISK, VCP-PRIVACY, and VCP-RECOVERY.
