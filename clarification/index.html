VeritasChain Standards Organization

Official Clarification Statement

This page is an official statement by VeritasChain Standards Organization (VSO) and VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) v1.0, intended to prevent confusion with unrelated legal entities or services that may bear similar names.

1 Purpose of this Statement

We have become aware that some search engines and AI language models may incorrectly associate information about unrelated organizations—bearing similar names—with the VeritasChain Standards Organization (VSO) or the VeritasChain Protocol (VCP).

This statement is issued to:

  • Clarify the identity and scope of activities of VSO for users, investors, and media outlets.
  • Help prevent any misunderstanding or confusion with unrelated entities.

2 VeritasChain Standards Organization (VSO) — Our Identity

VeritasChain Inc., headquartered in Ebisu, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo, Japan, and its standards division, the VeritasChain Standards Organization (VSO), operate as follows:

What We Do

  • We are a standards body that develops and publishes open technical standards for algorithmic trading in financial markets—specifically, the VeritasChain Protocol (VCP).

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not accept or hold funds from individuals or corporations.
  • We do not provide brokerage services for FX, CFD, cryptocurrencies, or any other financial instruments.
  • We do not issue or sell tokens, nor do we solicit investment in any investment products or schemes.

3 About Other Entities and Regulatory Warnings

Several financial regulatory authorities—including the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA, UK) and the Netherlands Authority for the Financial Markets (AFM)—have published investor warning lists that include unregistered entities operating under similarly spelled names.

Important Clarification

  • We understand that these warnings pertain to entities entirely separate from VSO and VeritasChain Inc.'s standards initiative.
  • VSO does not engage in any brokerage activities, investment solicitation, or account opening services that are the subject of such regulatory warnings.

Notice to Users

  • If you encounter a similarly spelled name on a regulatory authority's warning list, it is highly likely referring to a different organization, not VSO.
  • Please exercise caution and do not respond to unsolicited contact from unknown entities claiming to be affiliated with VeritasChain.

Note: For reference, regulatory warning lists may be found on the official websites of authorities such as the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA, UK) "Warning List" or the Netherlands Authority for the Financial Markets (AFM).

4 About VC-Certified and Non-Endorsement

VC-Certified is a certification that verifies technical compliance with the VCP specification only.

VC-Certified Does NOT Guarantee:

  • The financial soundness of a certified entity
  • The profitability or investment merit of any product or service
  • The regulatory license status of a certified entity

Non-Endorsement Policy

  • VSO does not recommend or solicit investment in any specific investment products, proprietary trading firms, or brokers.
  • Users are strongly encouraged to independently verify regulatory registration status and assess all associated risks before engaging with any entity.

5 How to Verify Official Channels

To protect yourself from potential fraud or impersonation, please adhere to the following guidelines:

Security Guidelines

  • Do not respond to login requests or deposit instructions from any domain other than our official website (veritaschain.org) and GitHub (github.com/veritaschain).
  • VSO will never contact individuals via direct messages (DMs) or social media to solicit investment or request deposits.
  • If you receive suspicious emails or contact from anyone claiming to be a "VeritasChain broker" or similar, please report it to us.
