4. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES

4.1 Core Functionality

VeraSnap is a cryptographic evidence camera application that implements the Content Provenance Protocol (CPP). The App provides:

  • Cryptographically signed capture events using device-generated keys
  • RFC 3161-compliant timestamps from external Time Stamping Authorities
  • Merkle tree batch aggregation for efficient verification
  • Privacy-first proof sharing capabilities
  • Optional Attested Capture Mode with biometric verification binding
  • Verification tools to validate proofs from the App or external sources

4.2 Important Limitations

YOU EXPRESSLY ACKNOWLEDGE AND AGREE THAT:

  • Provenance Does Not Equal Truth: The App proves WHEN media was captured and by WHAT device, but does NOT verify the authenticity or accuracy of the content itself. The App cannot determine whether what was photographed represents reality.
  • No Legal Guarantee: While the App generates cryptographic proofs, the admissibility or legal validity of such proofs varies by jurisdiction and is subject to the discretion of relevant authorities. We make no representations regarding legal validity.
  • Third-Party Dependencies: The App relies on external services including Time Stamping Authorities. We do not control these third-party services and cannot guarantee their continued availability, accuracy, or performance.
  • Network Requirements: Certain features, including RFC 3161 timestamping, require network connectivity. Offline captures will be timestamped when connectivity is restored.

5. PRIVACY AND DATA HANDLING

5.1 Privacy by Design

VeraSnap is designed with privacy as a core principle. All cryptographic operations, including key generation and signing, are performed locally on your device. Your Media and full Proof Data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers without your explicit action.

5.2 Data Processing

The App processes the following data:

  • Local Processing Only: Media files, cryptographic keys, signatures, and Proof Data are processed and stored exclusively on your device.
  • Hash Values Only: When obtaining RFC 3161 timestamps, only cryptographic hash values (not your actual Media or personal information) are transmitted to external Time Stamping Authorities.
  • Location Data (Optional): Location recording is disabled by default. If enabled, location data is stored locally within your Proof Data and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Biometric Data: For Attested Capture Mode, the App uses your device's biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID). We do NOT store, process, or have access to your biometric data. The App only records that authentication was successfully performed.

5.3 Data Deletion

You may delete your Media at any time. When deleting, you can choose to: (a) delete the Media file only while retaining the Proof Data, or (b) invalidate the Proof Data by creating a Tombstone record. This design philosophy—"Delete the data, but never delete the truth"—ensures provenance records remain verifiable even after Media deletion.
