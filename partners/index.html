Ways to Contribute

VSO welcomes technical contributions, research collaborations, and participation in standardization efforts. All contributions are open and vendor-neutral.

Technical Documentation

Contribute to VCP specification drafts, implementation guides, and technical documentation. All contributions are open-source and publicly accessible.

Research Collaboration

Participate in academic research projects related to algorithmic trading auditability, cryptographic verification, and regulatory compliance.

Public Consultation

Provide feedback on draft specifications through public consultation processes. All feedback is reviewed by the VSO Technical Committee.

Educational Events

Participate in workshops, webinars, and technical seminars organized by VSO. All events are non-commercial and education-focused.

Technical Contributions Welcome

VSO operates as a vendor-neutral, open standardization body. We welcome technical contributions, research feedback, and participation in public consultation processes. All contributions are reviewed through transparent, merit-based processes.
