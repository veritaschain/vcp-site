Our Mission

"Encoding Trust in the Algorithmic Age"

We realize transparency and verifiability in financial markets through cryptographic protocols, driving the paradigm shift from "Trust Me" to "Verify Me." In modern financial markets dominated by high-frequency trading and AI-driven algorithmic trading, our mission is to completely transparentize black-boxed trading processes and establish mathematically verifiable truth.

Corporate Information

Business Activities

Founding Philosophy

To establish transparency and trustworthiness in algorithmic trading through technical standards and build cryptographically verifiable financial market infrastructure

Vision

To make VCP the global standard for audit trails in financial markets, realizing the transition from "reputation-based trust" to "cryptographically verifiable truth"

VeritasChain Standards Organization (VSO)

Organization Type

International Standards Body & Certification Authority

Primary Responsibilities

Formulation of VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) technical specifications, management and operation of VC-Certified certification programs, implementation of conformance testing, publication of industry guidelines

International Position

Aspiring to be an organization with authority comparable to international standards bodies such as ISO, IEEE, and IETF, recognized by regulators, investors, and market participants

Stakeholders

Exchanges, brokers, proprietary trading firms, regulators, institutional investors, algorithm developer community

Organizational Structure

VeritasChain Inc.

Corporate Operating Entity

VSO

Standards & Certification Body

Organizational Relationship: VeritasChain Inc. provides operational foundation and support for VSO, while VSO operates as an independent standards body conducting neutral formulation of technical specifications and fair operation of VC-Certified certification programs. This separation structure ensures independence of commercial interests and standardization activities.

Non-Endorsement Policy: VSO operates as an independent standards body and does not endorse or promote any specific brokers, firms, or trading products. VC-Certified certification indicates compliance with transparency standards, not a recommendation of commercial services. VSO maintains strict vendor neutrality to serve the entire market ecosystem.
