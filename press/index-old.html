2. Executive Photos & Bios

Tokachi Kamimura

Founder, VeritasChain Standards Organization (VSO)

Tokachi Kamimura is the founder of the VeritasChain Standards Organization (VSO), an independent international standards body established in 2025 to promote transparency and accountability in algorithmic trading systems.

With extensive experience in financial technology and cryptographic systems, Kamimura-san developed the VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) v1.0 as the world's first open cryptographic audit standard for AI-driven and algorithmic trading.

His vision is to replace trust-based oversight with mathematically provable transparency, enabling regulators, auditors, and market participants to independently verify the integrity of algorithmic trading systems.
