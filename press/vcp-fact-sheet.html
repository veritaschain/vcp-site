VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) v1.0

The World's First Open Cryptographic Audit Standard for Algorithmic Trading

What is VCP?

VeritasChain Protocol (VCP) is an open cryptographic standard that creates tamper-evident, end-to-end verifiable audit trails for AI-driven and algorithmic trading systems. VCP replaces trust-based oversight with mathematically provable transparency, enabling independent verification without requiring access to proprietary systems.

Key Innovation: VCP uses cryptographic hash chains (SHA-256) and chronologically-ordered UUIDv7 timestamps to create immutable records that any third party can verify—from regulators to auditors to market participants—without trusting the record keeper.

Why This Matters Now

  • Trust Crisis in Algorithmic Trading: Recent scandals involving retail brokers, prop firms, and exchanges have eroded market confidence
  • Regulatory Pressure: MiFID II, SEC Rule 15c3-5, and FINRA 3110 demand stronger algorithmic oversight
  • AI Complexity: Black-box AI trading models make traditional audits insufficient
  • Global Market Need: No existing standard provides cryptographic-grade transparency for algorithmic systems

Technical Specifications

  • Hash Algorithm: SHA-256
  • Timestamp Format: UUIDv7 (RFC 9562)
  • Architecture: Merkle-tree hash chain
  • Verification: Independent third-party
  • Data Format: Structured JSON events
  • License: CC BY 4.0 (Open Standard)

Certification Tiers

  • Silver: Basic transparency (signal → execution)
  • Gold: Enhanced verification (+ risk management)
  • Platinum: Full auditability (+ model versioning)

Progressive compliance framework allows organizations to adopt VCP incrementally based on their needs.

Target Markets

Use Cases

  • Retail Brokers: Prove fair execution and absence of stop-loss hunting
  • Prop Trading Firms: Demonstrate transparent evaluation and payout systems
  • Cryptocurrency Exchanges: Verify order matching and prevent front-running
  • Hedge Funds: Provide auditable compliance for algorithmic strategies
  • Regulators: Enable efficient oversight without system access

Regulatory Alignment

VCP aligns with existing financial regulations: MiFID II (EU algorithmic trading requirements), SEC Rule 15c3-5 (Market Access Rule), FINRA 3110 (Supervision requirements), and emerging AI governance frameworks. Currently engaging with ISO/TC 68 (Financial Services) for potential standardization pathway.
