The Solution

VeritasChain Protocol (VCP)

An international standard for recording algorithmic trading decisions in an immutable and verifiable format.

VCP-CORE

Immutable Audit Trail

Creates cryptographically secured audit trails using Hash Chains and Merkle Trees. Every event is mathematically linked to its predecessors.

  • UUID v7 time-ordered identifiers
  • SHA-256 hash chains
  • Tamper-evident records

VCP-GOV

Algorithmic Explainability

Records governance metadata, versioning, and decision factors. Designed specifically for EU AI Act compliance.

  • AI decision logging
  • Version control for rules
  • Audit-ready documentation

VCP-RISK

Risk Parameter Snapshots

Captures risk parameters and rules at the exact moment of execution. Mathematical proof of consistent rule application.

  • Point-in-time snapshots
  • Rule enforcement proof
  • Dispute resolution data
